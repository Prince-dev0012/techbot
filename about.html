About TechXplore

The annual technical festival that brings together the brightest minds.

Our Mission

TechXplore aims to foster innovation, creativity, and technical excellence among students. Since its inception in 2015, it has grown to become one of the premier technical festivals in the region, hosting over 50 events, workshops, and guest lectures from industry leaders. We believe in learning by doing, and our events are designed to challenge participants to apply their knowledge to real-world problems.
